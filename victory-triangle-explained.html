🧠 What is the Victory Triangle?

The Victory Triangle is a mathematical framework that uses geometry and the Pythagorean Theorem to analyze sports matchups and predict game outcomes with statistically significant accuracy.

The three sides represent: Team Strength, Win Probability, and Momentum

Core Concept:
A² + B² = C² (Pythagorean Theorem Applied to Sports)

In Sports Terms:

  • A = Team Performance: Historical wins/losses ratio
  • B = Opponent Strength: Head-to-head advantage
  • C = Predicted Outcome: The winning probability
📐 Pythagorean Theorem in Sports

Baseball statistician Bill James discovered the Pythagorean Theorem accurately predicts win percentages. We've adapted this for ALL sports:

Win% = R² / (R² + OR²) Where: R = Team Runs Scored (Points in other sports) OR = Opponent Runs Allowed (Points allowed) Example: Team A scores: 120 points Team A allows: 100 points Win% = 120² / (120² + 100²) Win% = 14,400 / (14,400 + 10,000) Win% = 14,400 / 24,400 Win% = 0.591 = 59.1% VICTORY PROBABILITY ✓
